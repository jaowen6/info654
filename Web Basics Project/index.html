Introduction

Hello, my name is Jane. My intention for this project is to create a simple webpage and share some of my recent consumption habits including books, music, art, etc. I am in no way a qualified arbiter on any of these topics, but I have opinions, so here they are!

On getting offline

I am currently a Library and Information Science student at Pratt, new-ish to New York City, and like many people am trying to be more intentional about getting offline. Though the intention has been there for a while, this recent New York Times guest essay spurred a sense of urgency to stop complaining about my attention span and do something. My understanding of habit-making science is that it is much more effective to commit to healthy habits than to try to restrict unhealthy ones. Thus, in my effort to curb my phone addiction, I am aiming to intentionally consume other forms of media.

I have also been trying to spend time every week creating something. Here are some creative outlets I’ve been using to heal my brain:

  1. Learning to knit lace
  2. Experimenting with sourdough, including:
    1. Artisan loaves
    2. Bagels
    3. English Muffins
  3. Journaling
